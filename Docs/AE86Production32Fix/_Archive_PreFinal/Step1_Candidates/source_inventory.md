## Inventory conclusion

The only exact active-identity source art is `full ae86.png` and its 17 transparent extractions. The nine 1254x1254 legacy Body images are unreferenced and useful only as visual angle references: their scale, background quality, pixel density, and in several cases car construction differ from the active Production32 strip. `Car_Test.png` is a blank white texture referenced only by archived test-car prefabs. Audit/contact sheets are derivative composites and are not candidate sources.
